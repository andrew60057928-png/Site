Our Story

Tech Savvy Solutions was founded in Coleraine in 2018 with a single mission: bring enterprise-grade IT to local businesses without the enterprise price tag.

Seven years on, we support clients across hospitality, retail, sport and entertainment — proudly Northern Irish, proudly independent.

Our Mission

We make technology simple. From a single broken printer to a full network overhaul, we treat every job with the same care and clear communication.

No tech jargon. No surprise invoices. Just reliable systems and a number you can actually phone when something goes wrong.

Our Values

Honesty, craftsmanship and community sit at the centre of everything we do. We sponsor local sport, hire local talent and reinvest in the area we call home.

If your business is in or around the Causeway Coast, chances are we already know your neighbours.
