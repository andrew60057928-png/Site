Meet The Team

From our CEO Jake Newmans to head developer Leo Watkins, social media lead Ian Richards and employee manager Ivan Mcgrath, our four-strong core team has grown Tech Savvy from a Coleraine garage into one of the area's most trusted IT firms.

Between us we hold certifications in Cisco networking, Microsoft 365 administration, cybersecurity and modern web development. Every job that comes through the door is touched by someone who genuinely cares about getting it right.

Get to know the faces behind the company. Their backgrounds, their roles and what each of them brings to the table when you pick up the phone.

Services

We're proud to count Gareth's Takeaways and Coleraine F.C. among our long-standing clients, supporting their EPOS systems, network infrastructure, ticketing platforms and digital presence around the clock.

Our services span on-site IT support, structured cabling, server & cloud migration, EPOS deployment, cybersecurity audits, CCTV, website design and ongoing managed support contracts tailored to the size of your business.

Whether you're a takeaway opening a second site or a sports club rolling out a new ticketing portal, we scope it, build it and stay with you long after launch.

Our Values

Honesty, craftsmanship and community sit at the centre of everything we do. We sponsor local sport, hire local talent and reinvest in the area we call home.

We quote up-front, talk in plain English and never bill for time we didn't spend. If a job is smaller than we expected, the invoice shrinks too. And if something we installed fails inside its warranty, we're back out the same day to put it right.

If your business is in or around the Causeway Coast, chances are we already know your neighbours. We'd love to add you to the list.

MEET THE TEAM

Jake Newmans
CEO
28/09/1988

Jake founded Tech Savvy in 2018 after fifteen years working for large IT consultancies across Belfast and Dublin. He wanted to bring the same calibre of service to small businesses on the Causeway Coast at a price they could actually afford.

As CEO, he sets the company's direction, negotiates the bigger contracts and still personally handles every new client onboarding. Outside of work he's a keen sea kayaker and a vocal supporter of local apprenticeship schemes.

Leo Watkins
Head Developer
17/08/2003

Leo joined Tech Savvy straight out of Ulster University in 2022 and quickly worked his way up to Head Developer. He leads our web, mobile and EPOS development work and has a particular passion for clean, maintainable code.

His proudest project to date is the bespoke ticketing portal we built for Coleraine F.C., which now handles thousands of match-day transactions every season without breaking a sweat. When he's not coding, you'll find him modding mechanical keyboards.

Ian Richards
Social Media Manager
17/10/1994

Ian runs all of Tech Savvy's marketing, social channels and client communications. Since taking the role in 2021 he has tripled our Instagram following and built a content calendar that keeps the company visible across every platform that matters.

He's also our unofficial photographer, capturing the install jobs, charity nights and team events that fill the gallery page. Ian holds a Distinction in Digital Marketing from Belfast Met.

Ivan Mcgrath
Employee Manager
28/10/2001

Ivan looks after the people who look after our clients. As Employee Manager he runs recruitment, training, payroll and our apprenticeship programme, working closely with the local college to bring new talent through the door each year.

He has a CIPD Level 5 qualification and previously spent eight years in HR at a Belfast engineering firm. Ivan is the reason our staff turnover sits well below industry average and the reason new starters feel at home from day one.
